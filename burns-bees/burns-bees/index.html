Our Story

A small apiary, big on care

Burns Bees is a small, family-run apiary tucked in the countryside of Carp, Ontario. We keep just enough hives to know each one by name — tending them gently, harvesting slowly, and bottling honey the way it's meant to taste: raw, golden, and full of character.

Every jar and comb comes straight from our hives to your table, with nothing added and nothing taken away.
